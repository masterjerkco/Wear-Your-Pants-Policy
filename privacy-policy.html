Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view your personal data within the App, including your profile, photos, notes, and messages.

Delete Your Data

You can delete:

  • Individual progress photos from your Inventory
  • Individual journal notes
  • Your account (contact us at the email below)

Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this:

Method 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)
  1. Open the App and go to Squad page
  2. Tap on the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right
  3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account" button
  4. Enter your account password to verify your identity
  5. Type exactly "Yes I confirm" in the confirmation box
  6. Tap "Delete My Account" to permanently delete your account
Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to genew.studio@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (email, display name, avatar)
  • All progress photos stored in the cloud
  • All journal notes and difficulty ratings
  • Chat messages with friends
  • Streak data, credits, and achievements
  • Friend connections and requests
  • All purchased/owned items

This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
