Privacy Policy for Wear Your Pants!

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wear Your Pants! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Email and username (via Google Sign-In).
  • User Content: Progress photos (stored locally/cloud), journal notes, and chat messages.
  • Usage Data: Streak counts, credits earned, and item unlocks.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve relevant ads, Google may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • General location (Country/City)
  • App interaction data
Note: Premium subscribers enjoy a completely ad-free experience.

4. Data Security

Your data is secured using Supabase cloud encryption. Progress photos are private and are only shared with the friends you explicitly add.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings menu in the App. This will permanently remove your photos, notes, and profile.
